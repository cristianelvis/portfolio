The first step is to open a store on the platform.

Access the “Store Management” button in the left corner and log in (with the same data as social network access); You will then be in the “My Store” environment! Click on the “Stores” button and register by filling in the information.

With a store created, access the My Stores/Products menu. Click on the add button product; Fill in all the information and save. Your registered products will appear listed on the products page.

There are two ways to have your store on FreedomeE:

1. To create a store and sell your own products, you need a CNPJ. You can control the stock and will be responsible for shipping.

2. Only with the CPF it is possible to open a store. But in this case your store will be a Reseller of products from other stores.

This monitoring is made through the “Sales” screen. All orders are listed by status. You can check, for example, which are still pending and which already have payment confirmed.

Yea! Through your page on the social network, there is an access at the top of the page called “Shop”. By following it the page will open and the link or URL can be copied and shared.

There are basically 3 divisions:

Freedomee - Platform maintenance fee. (Varies by category. From 5 to 10%)

Commercial - Percentage used by people who resell their products (varies from 3 to 9%)

Marketing - Fixed percentage of 3% for philanthropy and 5% will be invested in marketing services. Example: Brand publicity, sharing advertising content created by you.

It will be done by the post office via PAC or SEDEX.

The period for receipt in your digital account is 30 days after confirmation of purchase payment. Check the balance in your FreedomeE Money Cloud account.

To perform any action that pays commission or to receive earnings from your store, is required to open a FreedomeE Money Cloud digital account.

At each commissioning point, there are mechanisms that collect the CPF and mobile number (documents necessary for opening an account). By filling and saving it is now possible see your account being created.

On first access, on the social network, through the general settings / earnings / my balance menu, it is possible to access your account and activate it.

Marketplace product photography guidelines

All products that are available for resale can be shared by any shopkeeper. To prevent a product from being shared by others, access my store/products choose the item you no longer want to share and under “allow resale” choose the option “No”. After saving the change, it will no longer be possible for your product to be shared by any store.
