Consumer

The first step for you to create prosperity is to use your purchasing power as a consumer.

What is your purchasing power?

By interacting within our Digital Platform, you choose the products, the brand and/or the entrepreneur who sells the products you identify with. To help you make better choices, FreedomeE.com has developed the 5 Positive Impact Mediators to inspire and direct your consumption choices. Within our collaborative economy platform, you act as a #FreedomeEUser Consumer when you make more conscious choices.

You get discounts when you consume and bonuses for using the platform.

PRODUCTIVITY EXCHANGE

There are several ways you can monetize on FreedomeE.com: through sales and/or generating bonuses and/or earning commission.

1. FreedomeE.com Solution

  • To monetize small and medium entrepreneurs.
  • For those who wish to earn extra income.

How can we help?

By connecting within our digital platform, we enable cooperation through “commercial interest groups” linking entrepreneurs with consumers, investors and philanthropists. Together these agents, in a multi-role model, promote products and services generating business and strengthening the “FreedomeE.com Productivity Exchange”.

Network of connections where entrepreneurs in their various profiles boost and cooperate with each other generating business opportunities in a continuous circular economy where everyone profits through the “Productivity Exchange”.

2. FreedomeE.com Productivity Exchange Sustainability

  • What is the Productivity Exchange?
    • It is a “Customer Loyalty Program” through the accumulation of bonuses that can be redeemed via discounts or “cash back”. The #FreedomeEUsers accumulate this bonus through the various interactions within the FreedomeE.com Digital Platform.
  • A visão do futuro
    • União da Tecnologia “FreedomeE.com + Moedas digitais com lastro” em países em desenvolvimento através de investidores de diferentes portes que investem em pequenos, médios e grandes empreendedores nas áreas de: ouro, comida, indústria e tecnologia.

Producer

The #FreedomeEUser Producer is that factory owner or small entrepreneur who owns the stock of products and is aligned with at least one of FreedomeE's 5 sustainable mediators.

How to take advantage of this opportunity?

As a producer, you can use different showcases to promote your products within the platform – such as marketplace, social commerce platform and commercial interest groups. Your products gain engagement by sellers, influencers and brand ambassadors within our ecosystem.

You produce your products and sell directly to the consumer.
