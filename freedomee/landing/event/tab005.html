Anna Paola Frade

Journalist and Editor

Brazil

She worked as a television presenter, but due to her preference for print media she founded her own magazine, Anna. She has always fought for the underprivileged and for the appreciation of women. “Hand made” is her passion and she has always preferred to use Brazilian products, made by artisans who incorporate art into fashion; and through the Special Editions of the ANNAs Magazines she promotes these artists. She promoted also Brazilian plastic arts and design in several areas.

At the age of 50, she became an Influencer and model for the L’ Model team in Florianópolis, and at 55, she works with beauty representing important brands. So she tries to encourage women in all ways and of all ages.

Bel Pimenta

PR • Relationship & Experience Marketing

Brazil

Bel started her career in the digital universe in 2015, organically. Communicative by nature and very easy to make new friends and get together different groups of people. Bel believes she has always done a little PR work in her daily life. With that in mind, in 2017, she proposed to the founding partners of List & Co., PR and Relationship Marketing Agency, a three-month working partnership that now lasts for another three years.

Throughout all this time, sharing a little of her daily life on social networks has always been very natural for Bel. In a simple way and without much production, his travels and his busy lifestyle with personal and customer events started to draw more and more attention from new followers. Nowadays, skin care, your maternity routine, fashion and lifestyle tips are also topics shared daily with those who follow your Instagram profile.

https://www.instagram.com/belpimenta/

Bruna Leo

Co-Founder FreedomeE

Brazil

Após se formar em direito pela FAAP (Fundação Armando Alvares Penteado), Bruna Leo passou a trabalhar por mais 15 anos no setor de tecnologia.

Dani Mello

Creative Director @sou.simple

Brazil

With a disruptive eye for the communication market, Dani established an innovative language for her content.

That's how she started to be successful on Instagram, sharing images and videos that tell in poetry about her style, beauty and lifestyle. Today, her network has loyal followers who are inspired by her sophisticated and modern style, as well as her beauty tricks.

Born in Curitiba, the creator has already gone through the great communication hubs Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo, where she is currently and lives with her husband and children. Dani also acts as Head of his own content agency, from which all the materials developed by the creator come out to transmit knowledge to those who want to embark on the multimedia life of social networks. In addition, Dani continues to undertake and is currently in charge of the Creative direction of a Wellness startup, Simple.

Fernanda Simon

Director of the Fashion Revolution Brazil Institute

Brazil

Fernanda Simon is the Executive Director of Instituto Fashion Revolution Brasil, the contributing sustainability editor for Vogue and columnist for Um Só Planeta. She studied fashion at the university Santa Marcelina and in England began her career with fashion and sustainability. She believes that fashion is a powerful tool for transformation and has been promoting this through various initiatives that promote fashion that is good for people and for the Planet.

Francesca Giobbi

Founder FreedomeE

Brazil

Formou-se em Arquitetura de Interiores pelo IED em Milão.
Giobbi é uma empresária com mais de 30 anos de experiência na indústria da moda internacional em relação ao desenvolvimento e distribuição global com operações na China, Índia, Brasil e Itália.

Em 2008 Giobbi conquistou o Prêmio Brazil Fashion de Melhor Designer de Acessórios de Moda. Após seis anos pesquisando e auditando in loco (Américas, Europa, Ásia e África) e nos principais centros produtivos e comerciais da moda global, ela decidiu lançar sua visão de gerar negócios de forma justa e sustentável, com foco na sustentabilidade no REINO UNIDO.

Idealizadora da Freedomee.com, plataforma de matchmaking econômico.

Maria Christina Mendes Caldeira

Founder Togerther4Earth

United States

Maria Christina Mendes Caldeira connects people, cultures and trends. She is a world citizen with deep intercultural knowledge of Brazil, USA and Middle East.

She uses Art as a tool to awaken and change the status quo.

She believes and advocates for more female leaders in Brazil as also participates in several global initiatives such as Emily’s list, Vital Voices, Care, Mulheres do Brasil and Bem querer mulher.

In 2021 she started Togerther4Earth to bring awareness to sustainability issues.
