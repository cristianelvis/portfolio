How will the event be?

A. Opening in the “House of Lords” of the English Parliament

B. 3 simultaneous stages with 5 panels based on the 5 mediators FreedomeE

Stage 1: Humanizing the Economy: How People Shape the Market

Stage 2: Humanizing Technology: Enabling and generating connections and opportunities

Stage 3: COLLABORATIVE CIRCULAR ECONOMY: Generating business between developed and underdeveloped countries

Valuing differences and encouraging participation, we bring more representation to society and achieve better results!

C. At the end, 5 COMERCIAL MATCHMAKING Rooms will be opened based on the 5 Mediators, with the objective of a constructive debate among the participants, aiming to find solutions to improve the human being's reality. The proposal is that this "brainstorm" comes out of effective actions so that they can be effectively developed in the next year.
