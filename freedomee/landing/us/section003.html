The 5 Mediators

New inclusive circular economy based on a union system that values workers, investing in Integrated, Cooperative, Collective and Inclusive entrepreneurship and consumption, and Cooperatives in all spheres of the chain, to create opportunities for economic development for all.

ECONOMIC

HUMAN

ANTHROPIC

TECHNOLOGICAL

Humanization of Technology

QUALITY

Transparency in Productive Relations

FreedomeE.com, more than a digital business platform, is a great movement based on 5 sustainable Mediators developed by founder Francesca Giobbi.

With the union of entrepreneurs, consumers, liberal professionals, philanthropists, educators, investors, politicians and diplomats, the 5 Mediators bring together in the same digital space, people and companies with the same vision, for them to produce and generate positive impacts through fair trade, collective and cooperative investments, bringing benefits to humanity through an Integrated Circular Economy, Cooperative, Collective and Inclusive.
