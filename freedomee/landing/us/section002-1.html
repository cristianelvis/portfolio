Who are we?

FreedomeE.com is your B2B2P2Ph2SI ECONOMIC MATCHMAKING PLATFORM that joins COMMERCIAL NETWORK and ONLINE STORE (Marktplace) in one place, where you share ideas, have fun, consume, invest, sell, indicate and produce; assuming your various roles as #FreedomeEUser.

FreedomeE.com potentializes its "networking" connecting people who share the same interests locally and globally, whether personal or professional, optimizing their time, valuing talent, generating online business in a dignified, sustainable and profitable way, through the various tools that the platform offers.

FreedomeE.com welcomes all profiles and is your ally to increase your profits and help you gain your financial freedom by building an Inclusive Circular Economy.

Click here and learn more about our Integrated, Cooperative, Collective, Inclusive Economy.

  • B2B = BUSINESS 2 BUSINESS – ENTREPRENEURS with stock of PRODUCTS and SERVICES connect with SELLERS generating business opportunities for those who produce and for those who sell.
  • 2P = 2 PEOPLE – ENTREPRENEURS sell their PRODUCTS and SERVICES directly to the end consumer, who has the opportunity to participate in this inclusive circular economy and benefits from discounts and financial gains by promoting these products and services.
  • 2Ph = 2 PHILANTHROPY - ENTREPRENEURS and CONSUMERS give back to the community while producing, selling, indicating, consuming, and investing; valuing the perception of the brand in society.
  • 2SI = 2 SOCIAL INVESTMENT – INVESTORS, ENTREPRENEURS, and CONSUMERS seek a positive social impact by investing in the INTEGRATED CIRCULAR ECONOMY Cooperative, Collective and Inclusive: GOLD, FOOD, INDUSTRY, COMMERCE, AND TECHNOLOGY.
  • CIRCULAR - Circular flows of reutilization, restoration and renovation, replacing the end-of-life concept of the linear economy.
  • INTEGRATED - Systems integrated within each other. Waste originates from non-integration between sectors of the production, distribution and communication chain.
  • COLLABORATIVE - All business links must cooperate with each other and not be based on competitive and/or exploitative relationships.
  • COLLECTIVE – Unity is strength! To be agents of real change, scale is essential.
  • INCLUSIVE - Economic development opportunities for all, regardless of ethnicity, social class, sex, gender, education, physical condition or economic situation
