About Us

Hi, I'm Frascesca Giobbi, founder of FreedomeE.

Selling products is not just about making a profit.

In addition to the product and commercial solution you offer, the platform encourages your customers to engage in the cause you support through the 5 mediators.

When signing up, you will choose the mediator you would like to be a part of. Meet the mediators below:

Economic
Respect for Workers

This mediator is for everyone who wants to foment a new economy. More collaborative and based on a system that values entrepreneurship and workers, in all spheres of the chain. In this way, we create opportunities for economic development for all.

Human
Valuing the Human Being

This mediator is for everyone who contributes positively to the eradication of exploratory practices. They invest in education and respect cultural diversity and the “MADE-IN's” of each country.

Anthropic
Respect for Natural Resources

This mediator is for everyone who implements smart and more conscious processes in the creative, production, communication, commercial and logistics areas. Less waste and better use of natural resources.

Technological
Humanization of Technology

This mediator is for everyone who uses technology as a resource that should favor human development. New technologies that provide training, connection and opportunities for everyone.

Quality
Transparency in Productive Relationships

This mediator is for everyone who guarantees the quality of products through the transparency of information on the origin of the products and ethical human relations of production, consumption and influence.

The 5 Mediators

FreedomeE.com, more than a digital business platform, is a movement based on 5 sustainable mediators developed by founder Francesca Giobbi.

The 5 Mediators bring together, in the same digital space, people and companies with the same vision, so that they produce and generate more positive impacts for the world. Through a Collaborative Economy we unite entrepreneurs, consumers, professionals, philanthropists, educators, investors, politicians and diplomats around common values.

I'm Bruna Léo, co-founder of FreedomeE. You can participate in different ways on our platform and have 4 types of income:

Active: Placing products on the platform
Passive: Calling companies to join our ecosystem
Collaborative: Interacting with products and promoting new economy brands. Communicate, represent and influence.
Social: Every transaction on our platform, we donate 3% to philanthropy.

Important: the more you interact, the more money you earn!
